About Green Grocers

We are an online company that focuses on bring food directly to consumers instead of sending them to grocery stores By bringing food from the suppliers (farmers) directly to the consumers, it eliminates the cost of sending those products to wholesalers, retailers, and thus we can sell food to consumers at a discounted price.

Who Is Green Grocers For?

Green Grocers is a service for those who have difficulty accessing grocery stores, or those with financial needs
